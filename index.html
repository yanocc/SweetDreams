Hi there! I'm Ouie, the heart behind Sweet Dreams!

Ever since I was young, I've had a big love for baking—mixing batter, sneaking tastes of frosting, and dreaming of having my own little bakery one day. But as I grew older, I slowly lost touch with that passion. For a while, I forgot that my dream was to become a baker.

Now, I've finally found the courage to start small and dream big again—and that's how Sweet Dreams was born! ⏾

Why the name? Well... I sell sweet goodies (of course!), I'm a big dreamer, and I absolutely love to sleep (seriously, naps are my thing ᶻ 𝗓 𐰁).

Sweet Dreams just felt like the perfect name for this cozy little dream of mine. I hope our sweets bring you comfort, joy, and a gentle reminder to keep chasing your dreams too. ⋆˙⟡
